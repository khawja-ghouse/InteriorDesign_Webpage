About

WOODMIND INTERIORS

With over 20 years of experience in the field, we specialize in complete wood furniture and interior design solutions for both entire buildings and individual flats across Bangalore.

Whether you're furnishing a new home or renovating your existing space, we provide end-to-end services tailored to your needs. Our expert team of 10–15 skilled professionals ensures top-quality workmanship and attention to detail in every project.

Our Service Models

  • Labour Contract: You provide the materials, we handle the design and execution.
  • Material Contract (Material + Labour): We source materials through our trusted dealer network—ensuring better pricing and quality—while also taking care of the complete implementation.

Choose designs from our wide collection of designs or bring your custom ideas—we'll bring them to life. We are proud to be a local, reliable interior and woodwork partner trusted by hundreds of customers over the past two decades.
